🏗️ System Architecture

Three-layer system that runs 24/7 on your Oracle Cloud VM:

Layer 1 — Structural Filter (runs weekly)
Scores top 100 coins by market cap on S/R zone suitability. Passes ~51 coins into the trading universe. Uses metrics: zone bounce rate (+4.5% edge), zone density, volume consistency.
Layer 2 — D2 Volume Rotation (runs every 4H)
Re-ranks the 51 coins by 4H volume. Selects top 35 for the active pool. Mid-week churn swaps stale coins (no trades in 672 bars + <2 total) for fresh ones showing volume spikes.
Layer 3 — Trading Engine (runs every 15m)
Checks pool coins for zone bounce entries. Trailing stop at 0.80R activation / 0.50R width. Time exit at 192 bars. RSI 30-70 guard. BTC correlation filter. Max 12 concurrent positions.
